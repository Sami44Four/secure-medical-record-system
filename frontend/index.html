Portal Overview

This final release focuses on secure medical record management, including authenticated access, role-based medical record visibility, protected upload validation, persistent audit logging, and AI-assisted security review for administrators.

Records retention policy: Patient medical records are retained according to Washington State healthcare data retention guidelines and HIPAA compliance requirements.
